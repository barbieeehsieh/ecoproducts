BELL

Bath Bomb

"This winter, stave off the darkness by reaching for this show-stopping Amazeball when you’re in a need of a bathtime treat to help you make it to the New Year. Make a wish on white sugar crystals and breathe in floral ylang ylang, comforting benzoin, and sweet almond essential oil as it all fizzes away in your bath. Don’t let the bells end, just let them ring in peace."

Ingredients:

  • Sodium Bicarbonate
  • Citric Acid
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Disodium Lauryl Sulfosuccinate
  • Propylene Glycol
  • White Sugar Crystals
  • Perfume
  • Ylang Ylang Oil
  • Benzoin Resinoid
  • Almond Essential Oil

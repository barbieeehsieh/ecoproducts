Charity pot

Philanthropic skin softener

This rich and generous cream contains seven ingredients sourced from our Sustainable Lush Fund projects. With every purchase of Charity Pot, we donate 100% of the price to small grassroots organizations working in the areas of human rights, animal protection and environmental justice.

Ingredients:

  • Water (Aqua)
  • Fair Trade Olive Oil (Olea europaea) 
  • Glycerine 
  • Organic Jojoba Oil
  • Moringa Oil (Moringa oleifera)
  •  Fair Trade Cocoa Butter
  •  Stearic Acid (Stearic acid) 
  • Fair Trade Shea Butte
  •  Fresh Aloe Gel

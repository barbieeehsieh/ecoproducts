LORD OF MISRULE

Bath Bomb

"Revel in the bath with this mischievous bomb that features a spicy herbal blend of patchouli and black pepper oil. Inspired by the ruler of the pagan Feast of Fools, Lord Of Misrule has a deep green exterior that slowly unfurls to reveal a rich wine-colored center. "

Ingredients:

  • Sodium Bicarbonate
  • Citric Acid
  • Popping Candy (Isomalt, Lycasin, Carbon Dioxide)
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Black Pepper Oil
  • Patchouli Oil
  • Perfume
  • Vanilla Absolutel
  • Bergamot Oil
  • Glycerine
